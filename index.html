I Wonder Why? Week

To inculcate logical thinking, problem solving ability and to develop scientific attitude among children, I Wonder Why? Week from 07.11.22 - 11.11.22 was celebrated. National Unity Day

Celebrating the National Unity Day on 31.10.22 , they enjoyed the informative session about Sardar Vallabhai Patel on his birth anniversary .Students were thrilled and inspired by the events that took place in his life and also his contribution towards our country.

Thanksgiving celerated by class 3 EFA

Thanksgiving

On 24th, the last Thursday of November, students learned about the history of the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade paired with reading a prayer from their English reader for Class 3. After reading, students identified some of the different ways Americans celebrate Thanksgiving. Next, students worked in small groups to name all the reasons they are thankful





Christmas

All Christmasyyy!


The festival of love, caring and sharing came alive at The Class of One on 23rd December 2022 when our students conducted a special assembly on Christmas. It was a fun- filled day with dance, christmas craft and game sessions. Students from different cultures participated in the celebration singing of Christmas carols in a cheerful spirit. During the festive time, the students dedicated Christmas messages to their loved ones. Some shared their year-end reflections while others indulged in optimistic mood for new year’s resolutions!
